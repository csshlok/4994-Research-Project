            Needs work to compete with B4, others

            Domain consultant
            Current employee, more than 1 year
            Los Angeles, CA
            Recommend
            CEO approval
            Business outlook

            Pros

            Less demanding on W/L balance than other big consulting firms No up-or-out policy Decent client base Big company with considerable resources Benefits okay (in particular my health insurance experience has been a plus) They *love* running in and supporting marathons, if thats your kind of thing

            Cons

            Partially because of size and partially due to being very india-centric, firm communications are very cluttered, busy, and simply prolific in quantity to the point of getting lost in the noise They're cheap, at every level. From pay/bonuses, to staffing projects, to agreeing to projects with minimal profitability (even before change orders and issues come up). Extremely annoying focus on white paper creation, PoV publication, and so forth as part of annual review. This is compounded by the fact the firm gives little to no guidance on exactly what they want in such white papers / PoVs / etc Tech support is very nearly nonexistent. It took me over a month to get access to my email (in 2023!!!) on my laptop. Compared to firms like Deloitte, the help desk is more likely to tell you that your support request was filled out wrong rather than solve your problem. Also simply getting a hold of them can be an absolute chore (and not having a 24/7 tech support phone number is a pretty big issue in consulting).
